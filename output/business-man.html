As a Business Man, What do You Do?

An African #business man who was being interviewed about his profession, responded this way to the question: "As a #business man sir, what do you do?" I do #business I #deal on #deals If you have a #gooddeal You get it to me If I #like it I #buy your #deal If you have #money to #buy my own I give you my #bill You #pay And that's #business
