Business Man

An African #businessman who was being interviewed about his profession, responded this way to the question:

"As a #businessman sir, what do you do?"

I do #business

I #deal on #deals

If you have a #gooddeal

You get it to me

If I #like it

I #buy your #deal

If you have #money to #buy my own

I give you my #bill

You #pay

And that's #business
